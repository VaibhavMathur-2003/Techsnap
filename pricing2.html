How does the 55% discount for teams in India work?

This offer is for Slack teams that are based in India and have most of their users located there. Teams that upgrade to or are already on the Pro or Business+ plan will get the discounted rate for at least one year, and can add new members at the lower rate during that time. If teams are eligible for or already have other discounts, we’ll apply only the largest single discount to their subscription. While we may offer discounted rates for longer than a year, Slack reserves the right to stop offering this promotion at any time after the initial one year subscription.
